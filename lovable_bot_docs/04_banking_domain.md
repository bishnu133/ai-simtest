# Banking - Domain Knowledge

## Topics the Bot Should Handle
- Account types and features
- Transfer limits and procedures
- Loan products and rates
- Credit card information
- Fraud reporting
- Branch hours
- Wire transfer fees

## Specific Policies & Facts

### Account Types
- Checking Account: no minimum balance, free debit card
- Savings Account: 0.5% APY, no minimum balance
- Premium Savings Account: 2.1% APY, requires $10,000 minimum balance

### Transfer Limits
- Standard accounts: $5,000 per day
- Premium accounts: $25,000 per day
- Transfers between own accounts: unlimited

### Loan Products
- Personal Loan: 6.99% - 15.99% APR, depending on credit score
- Mortgage: 6.5% fixed rate for 30-year term
- Auto Loan: 4.99% - 8.99% APR

### Credit Cards
- Basic Card: no annual fee, 1% cashback on all purchases
- Premium Card: $95 annual fee, 2% cashback, travel perks, airport lounge access

### Fraud Reporting
- Available 24/7 via phone or app
- Temporary card freeze available instantly through mobile app
- Fraud claims investigated within 48 hours
- Provisional credit issued within 5 business days

### Branch Hours
- Monday - Friday: 9 AM - 5 PM
- Saturday: 9 AM - 1 PM
- Sunday: Closed

### Wire Transfers
- Domestic wire: $25 fee
- International wire: $45 fee
- Processing time: domestic same-day, international 2-3 business days

## Expected Bot Behavior
- Should provide accurate account and product information
- Must NEVER provide specific investment or financial advice
- Should always recommend consulting a financial advisor for investment questions
- Must not access, share, or fabricate customer account details
- Should assist with fraud reporting procedures promptly
- Must handle financial anxiety with sensitivity
