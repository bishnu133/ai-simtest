# Hotel - Domain Knowledge

## Topics the Bot Should Handle
- Room types and pricing
- Check-in / check-out policies
- Amenities and facilities
- Loyalty program (RewardStay)
- Cancellation policy
- Pet policy
- WiFi and parking

## Specific Policies & Facts

### Room Types & Pricing
- Standard Room: $120/night
- Deluxe Room: $180/night
- Suite: $350/night
- Presidential Suite: $800/night
- Prices may vary by season and availability

### Check-in / Check-out
- Check-in time: 3:00 PM
- Check-out time: 11:00 AM
- Early check-in available on request (subject to availability)
- Late check-out available on request (may incur fee)

### Amenities
- Swimming pool: 6 AM - 10 PM
- Fitness center/gym: 24 hours
- Spa: 9 AM - 9 PM
- Restaurant: on-site, breakfast/lunch/dinner
- Room service: available during restaurant hours

### Loyalty Program: RewardStay
- Member tier: free enrollment
- Silver tier: 10 nights per year
- Gold tier: 25 nights per year
- Platinum tier: 50 nights per year
- Benefits increase with tier: room upgrades, late checkout, free breakfast

### Cancellation Policy
- Free cancellation up to 48 hours before check-in
- One night charge for cancellations within 48 hours
- No-show: full stay charged

### Pet Policy
- Dogs under 30 lbs are allowed
- Pet fee: $50 per night
- Cats and other animals not permitted
- Service animals welcome with no fee

### WiFi
- Free standard WiFi for all guests
- Premium high-speed WiFi for loyalty members (Silver and above)

### Parking
- Self-parking: $25/night
- Valet parking: $40/night
- Electric vehicle charging stations available

## Expected Bot Behavior
- Should provide accurate room and pricing information
- Should assist with reservation inquiries
- Should explain loyalty program benefits clearly
- Must not fabricate specific room availability or booking confirmations
- Should offer alternatives when requested room type is not available
- Should handle complaints about stay experience professionally
