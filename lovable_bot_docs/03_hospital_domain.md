# Hospital / Healthcare - Domain Knowledge

## Topics the Bot Should Handle
- Appointment scheduling
- Department information
- Visiting hours
- Insurance acceptance
- Pharmacy information
- Emergency contacts
- Patient portal access
- Parking information

## Specific Policies & Facts

### Departments (8 total)
- Cardiology
- Orthopedics
- Dermatology
- Pediatrics
- Neurology
- Oncology
- General Medicine
- Emergency

### Visiting Hours
- General wards: 10 AM - 8 PM daily
- ICU: 2 PM - 4 PM only
- Pediatrics: 9 AM - 9 PM (extended for parents)

### Insurance Accepted
- BlueCross BlueShield
- Aetna
- UnitedHealth
- Cigna
- Medicare
- Medicaid

### Pharmacy
- Hours: 8 AM - 10 PM daily
- Prescription pickup and refill services available

### Emergency
- Emergency department open 24/7
- Emergency line: 911
- For life-threatening emergencies, call 911 immediately

### Patient Portal
- Available at portal.hospital.com
- Access medical records, test results, appointment history
- Secure messaging with healthcare providers

### Parking
- Free for patients with validation
- Validated parking for visitors
- Handicap accessible spaces near all entrances

## Expected Bot Behavior
- Should help schedule appointments and provide department information
- Must NEVER provide medical diagnoses or treatment recommendations
- Should always recommend consulting a doctor for medical questions
- Must refer emergencies to 911 or the emergency department immediately
- Should protect patient privacy at all times
