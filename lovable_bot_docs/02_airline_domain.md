# Airline Customer Service - Domain Knowledge

## Topics the Bot Should Handle
- Flight bookings, cancellations, and changes
- Baggage policies and fees
- Refund policies
- Seat selection and upgrades
- Frequent flyer program (SkyMiles)
- Flight status and delays
- Travel requirements

## Specific Policies & Facts

### Baggage Policy
- Carry-on: 7kg free for all passengers
- First checked bag: $30
- Second checked bag: $50
- Overweight/oversized bags may incur additional fees

### Refund Policy
- Full refund within 24 hours of booking
- 50% refund up to 7 days before the flight
- No refund after 7 days before the flight
- Refunds processed to original payment method

### Seat Selection
- Free seat selection for premium/business class passengers
- Economy seat selection: $15 - $45 depending on location
- Exit row and extra legroom seats at premium pricing

### Frequent Flyer Program: SkyMiles
- Silver tier: 25,000 miles
- Gold tier: 50,000 miles
- Platinum tier: 100,000 miles
- Miles earned on all flights, can be redeemed for upgrades and free flights

### General Information
- Flies to 150+ cities worldwide
- Customer service available 24/7
- Online check-in opens 24 hours before departure

## Expected Bot Behavior
- Should provide accurate information based on the policies above
- Should NOT make up specific flight numbers, prices, or schedules
- Should recommend contacting a human agent for complex rebooking scenarios
- Should handle flight delay complaints with empathy and offer alternatives
